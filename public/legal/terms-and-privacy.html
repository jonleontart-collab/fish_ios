Terms, Privacy, and Safety Notice

This document is a product-ready legal draft for the FishFlow application. It is designed to set clear expectations about user-generated content, maps, messages, catch reports, and personal data. It is not legal advice and should be reviewed by qualified counsel before public launch.

FishFlow is a community platform. Information shown in the app, including weather summaries, route suggestions, water analysis, catches, chat messages, and place information, may be incomplete, delayed, inaccurate, or unsafe to rely on in isolation.

You must be at least 13 years old to create or use an account. By registering, you confirm that the date of birth you provide is accurate and that you are legally allowed to use the service in your country or region.

2. Nature of the Service

FishFlow provides social, discovery, and planning tools for anglers, including profiles, chat, media posts, inventory tracking, location discovery, trip planning, and AI-assisted summaries. The service is provided on an “as is” and “as available” basis.

3. No Safety, Navigation, Medical, Rescue, or Professional Advice

FishFlow is not a rescue service, navigation authority, government warning system, weather authority, or professional guide. Do not rely on the app as the sole basis for decisions involving water safety, boating, travel, weather exposure, equipment suitability, legal compliance, protected areas, or emergency response.

You are responsible for all content you upload or send, including photos, videos, place submissions, comments, messages, inventory items, trip reports, and reposts. You may not use FishFlow to harass, threaten, impersonate, spam, scam, distribute malware, promote illegal activity, publish explicit content, or organize unsafe conduct. Support and system chats may be preserved, prioritized, or restricted for operational and moderation reasons.

Place cards, discovery results, chat previews, AI analyses, and feed recommendations may be generated or ranked automatically from third-party data, public sources, or user submissions. We do not guarantee that any location is legal to access, currently open, productive, safe, or accurately described.

8. Account Suspension and Removal

We may suspend, limit, or permanently remove accounts or content where reasonably necessary for safety, abuse prevention, fraud control, moderation, legal compliance, operational integrity, or community trust.

9. Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of data:

  • Account data such as name, nickname, date of birth, language, and password hash.
  • Profile data such as bio, city, home water, inventory, banner, and avatar.
  • Content and messaging data including uploads, reposts, comments, and support requests.
  • Operational data such as cookies, device/browser signals, and log data needed for security.

10. Privacy Principles

FishFlow should process personal data only for specified service, security, moderation, support, and analytics purposes. Data should be limited to what is necessary, kept accurate where reasonably possible, and retained only for as long as needed for the stated purpose, legal obligations, dispute handling, or abuse prevention. We may use service providers and infrastructure partners on a need-to-know basis.

Account and content data may remain active while your account is active. Backup copies, moderation records, fraud-prevention logs, and legally required records may be retained longer where justified. Deleted content may persist for a limited time in caches, logs, or backups before full removal.

12. Your Controls

Subject to applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, export, or restriction of certain personal data. Some data may need to be retained for legal, security, billing, moderation, or anti-abuse reasons.

13. Children and Age Gating

FishFlow is not intended for children under 13. If we obtain actual knowledge that personal data was collected from a child below that threshold without the legally required basis, we may delete the account and associated data or request additional verification before access continues.

14. International Use

FishFlow may be operated, hosted, or supported from different jurisdictions. By using the service, you understand that your data may be processed in countries with different legal regimes, subject to applicable safeguards and operational requirements.

15. Contact

For support, moderation, privacy, or account concerns, contact the FishFlow support channel inside the app. If a public contact mailbox is added later, this document should be updated accordingly.

16. Source Notes

This draft reflects high-level principles from official privacy sources such as the European Commission’s GDPR guidance on data minimisation and storage limitation, and the U.S. FTC’s COPPA materials regarding services with actual knowledge of users under 13.
